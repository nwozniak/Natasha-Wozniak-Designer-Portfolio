Non-profit website

Website Design and Implementation

Strategy

The rebuilding project was very complex, so the first task was to compile all of the possible information and organize it into a narrative. After feedback from a variety of experts and the board, the content was edited down to the most relevant information for potential donors. The non-profit also needed to have integration with a donor database to organize fundraising efforts.

contact/ about

Website Design and Implementation

Design

Photos were edited and selected to promote the narrative and using brand colors and fonts provided by brand consultant, Tania Marie Roulston. I used these to create individual graphics and text content, using Squarespace. These photos and concepts also became posters and social media graphics, providing a consistent theme. The donation form was linked to the main database of the organization so that information about online donors could be automatically entered and incorporated, allowing for easy use of the donor data in future campaigns.
